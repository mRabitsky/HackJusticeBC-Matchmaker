Calendar and Feed
Calendar

Both the lawyers and clients will have access to a view of the calendar, where lawyers will have the ability to post their times, and clients will be able to select time slots that work for them.

Additionally, clients will have the ability to apply some filters, such as price range, availability, or general location.

Feed

Clients will have two methods by which they can post their cases: they will either click on a specific time slot and directly message a lawyer about the details of their case, or they can choose to post their case on a 'board' that any lawyer can see.

Lawyers will have a dashboard showcasing their current cases, as well as a feed that will show them any messages sent regarding their time slots.
